Kolkata Neighbourhoods

Welcome to Kolkata Neighborhoods!

Kolkata, the ‘Cultural Capital of India’ is the most unique metropolis of the country. Vibrant and ideal, modern yet traditional, Kolkata intriguingly juxtaposes itself and the Hooghly River slowly ebbs and flows to match its rhythm to the city.

Kolkata is where the rich Indian culture manifests itself wholly. Its traditional heritage fuses seamlessly with the remnants of its colonial past, adding a nostalgic charm to the city. This idiosyncrasy of Kolkata coupled with ancient landmarks, museums, shopping streets and delectable cuisine has a magnetic pull over travel enthusiasts who visit the city to experience its vibrant culture.
